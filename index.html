Racing Car 3D Multiplayer

How to Play

Race against time or other players on procedurally generated tracks. Master the perfect racing line and collect power-ups to boost your performance.

Controls

W/↑ Accelerate
S/↓ Brake/Reverse
A/← Steer Left
D/→ Steer Right
SPACE Handbrake
SHIFT Nitro Boost

Pro Tips

  • Use nitro boost on straight sections to maximize speed
  • Drift through corners by combining handbrake with steering
  • Watch your tire temperature - overheating reduces grip
  • Collect blue orbs for extra nitro boost
  • Use slipstream behind other cars to gain speed

About the Game

Polytrack is a next-generation racing game that combines stunning visuals with precise physics simulation. Race through dynamically generated tracks that ensure every race is unique. Compete in single-player time trials or challenge other players in intense multiplayer races.
